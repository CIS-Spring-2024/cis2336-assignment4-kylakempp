About Koda Kafe

Koda Kafe is more than just a cafe; it's a vibrant community hub located at the heart of the University of Houston Sugar Land campus.

Our mission is to provide students, faculty, and staff with a welcoming environment where they can study, socialize, and recharge. With comfortable seating areas, delicious food and beverages, and a commitment to affordability, we aim to create a home away from home for everyone in the UH Sugar Land community.

Our Goals

At Koda Kafe, our primary goal is to support the academic and social needs of students. We strive to:

  • Provide a comfortable and inviting space for studying and collaboration
  • Offer a variety of affordable and nutritious food options to fuel students' minds and bodies
  • Promote community engagement and foster connections among students, faculty, and staff
  • Support student success by offering job opportunities and practical experience through employment at the cafe

Job Opportunities

Looking for a job that fits your schedule and allows you to gain valuable experience while earning extra income? Join the Koda Kafe team!

We offer flexible part-time positions for students and staff in various roles, including cashiers, kitchen assistants, and custodians. Whether you're looking to develop your customer service skills, gain experience in the food service industry, or simply earn some extra cash, we have opportunities for you.

Join us at Koda Kafe and become part of a dynamic team dedicated to serving the UH Sugar Land community!
